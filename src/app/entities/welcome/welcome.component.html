About Us

Robo-Werk is a startup company created by students of TU Chemnitz. In this era of digital transformation, yet, there are many businesses needed user interaction to complete the similar task repeatedly. For this type of task, Robo-Werk is specialized in providing solutions with Robotic Process Automation to replace the repetitive efforts of users by automation. The main focus of Robo-Werk is to provide services that needs less human interaction and more self-execution.
